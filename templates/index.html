An AI agent is an autonomous software system that perceives its environment, makes decisions, and takes actions to achieve a goal — without constant human instruction. Unlike a simple chatbot that follows a fixed script, an AI agent can access your CRM, send emails, update databases, book appointments, and escalate to humans when needed. It works across multiple tools and adapts to new situations. Think of it as a digital employee with a job description, not a FAQ page with buttons.

Enterprise-grade multi-agent automation starts from ₹1.5L setup. Most clients recover their investment in the first 4–8 weeks. We always share a detailed ROI projection before any engagement begins.

n8n is an open-source workflow automation platform — like Zapier but far more powerful, self-hostable, and cost-effective at scale. We use n8n to build complex business automation workflows that connect your CRM, WhatsApp, email, ERP, spreadsheets, and AI models. Because it's self-hosted, your data stays on your servers, making it ideal for Indian businesses with data compliance requirements. n8n gives us 400+ native integrations to work with.
UiPath is the world's leading Robotic Process Automation (RPA) platform. It's ideal when you need to automate work on legacy systems, desktop applications, or web interfaces that don't have APIs — for example, copying data between an old ERP and a new system, automating government portal submissions, or processing documents in enterprise software. For API-connected systems, we typically recommend n8n or custom AI agents instead, which are faster and more cost-effective.
AI support agents resolve 70–85% of Tier-1 queries instantly — order status, FAQs, refund policies, appointment bookings — without any human involvement. For the remaining complex cases, the AI collects all context before routing to a human agent, so your team spends zero time on "where is my order?" calls. The result: faster response times (seconds, not hours), lower support costs, higher CSAT scores, and a support team that focuses only on cases that genuinely need a human mind.
