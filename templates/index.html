An AI employee is a software system that handles a defined set of business tasks — like a human employee would — but autonomously, 24/7, at a fraction of the cost. It can respond to customers, process documents, send emails, update your CRM, generate reports, and much more. Unlike RPA (which follows rigid rules), AI employees can reason, understand natural language, and adapt to new situations.
A chatbot follows pre-defined decision trees — it can only handle questions you've explicitly programmed for. An AI employee uses large language models to understand intent, context and nuance — it can handle questions it's never seen before, take multi-step actions across systems (updating your CRM, sending an email, booking a calendar slot), and escalate intelligently when something is outside its scope.
