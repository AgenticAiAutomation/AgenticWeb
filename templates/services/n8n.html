n8n vs Alternatives

Why n8n over Zapier or Make?

For Indian businesses handling sensitive data or high transaction volumes, n8n is the clear choice.

Data sovereignty: Self-hosted n8n keeps all your data on your own servers — critical for BFSI, healthcare and government clients

Cost at scale: Zapier charges per task — at 10,000 tasks/month, n8n is 10× cheaper with no incremental cost

Custom logic: n8n supports full JavaScript/Python in workflows, enabling complex business rules Zapier can't handle

AI-native: Built-in LLM nodes let you connect Claude, GPT-4, or local models directly into any workflow step

No vendor lock-in: Open-source means your workflows are yours forever — no platform dependency

Indian integrations: We've built custom n8n nodes for Razorpay, Zoho, Tally API, and Indian GST portals
